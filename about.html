RED SOIL TRADING HARDWARE

Red Soil Trading Hardware was established in 2014 to provide quality construction materials, tools, and hardware supplies at competitive prices. Backed by a team of skilled and dedicated workers, including sales specialists, logistics staff, and customer service representatives, the company ensures efficient service and customer satisfaction.

Meet the Owner of Red Soil Trading

Marlon Pabaya

Marlon Pabaya is a determined and hardworking entrepreneur who owns a successful hardware store in Mapulang Lupa, Valenzuela City. Graduated with a degree in Civil Engineering in Manuel L. Quezon University, Marlon did not pursue the licensure exam. Instead, they chose to apply their expertise in a different way by establishing a hardware store that caters to local builders, contractors, and homeowner
